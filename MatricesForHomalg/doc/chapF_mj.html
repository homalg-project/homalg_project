Table: The MatricesForHomalg package files (continued)

F.5 The homalgTable for GAP4 built-in rings

For the purposes of homalg, the ring of integers is, at least up till now, the only ring which is properly supported in GAP4. The GAP4 built-in cababilities for polynomial rings (also univariate) and group rings do not statisfy the minimum requirements of homalg.
